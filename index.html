A quick lay of the land

You're focused on the corridor between the Valley and town — Palmer, Eagle River, Meadow Lakes, and Chugiak. Palmer averages around $380K, while Eagle River and Chugiak run higher (often $400K–$450K+), so a $350K budget stretches furthest in Palmer and Meadow Lakes. Push toward $450K and Eagle River & Chugiak open up with mountain views and quick access to Anchorage.
