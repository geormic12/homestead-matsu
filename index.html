A quick lay of the land

The Mat-Su Valley sits about 45 minutes north of Anchorage. Wasilla and Palmer are the two main hubs — average home values run roughly $358K in Wasilla and $380K in Palmer, so a $350K budget is right in the sweet spot, especially in Wasilla, Big Lake, and Houston. Push toward $400K and Palmer opens up; drop toward $300K and you'll find cabins, smaller homes, and great lots.
